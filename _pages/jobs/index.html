PlaceWise Media got its start about 17 years ago as MallFinder.  Since then, we’ve grown to be the leading national digital marketing services provider to the shopping center industry and affiliate partner to hundreds of grocery retailers.  But you can read about that elsewhere on our site.  What you really want to know is what it’s like to work at PlaceWise.

We are a collaborative, flexible, creative team (ask us about our infamous annual white elephant gift exchange). We’re an active crew – lots of bike-riders in the house – and we love being a part of the action downtown. There are sports fans, movie buffs and more than a few musicians in our midst. No corporate drones here – our executive team encourages individuality, an entrepreneurial spirit, and provides opportunity for big thinkers who want to make a difference in the company.  The pace is fast and the vibe is dynamic and stimulating!

As an equal opportunity employer, we hire without consideration to race, religion, national origin, age, gender, sexual orientation, marital status, veteran status or disability.
